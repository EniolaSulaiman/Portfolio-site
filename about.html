    My Story

    I Started as a Builder.
    Data Made Me a Strategist.

    I'm Eniola Sulaiman — a frontend developer turned analytics and conversion specialist. I started out building websites and quickly realised that a beautiful site means nothing if it doesn't convert. That gap between design and results became my obsession.

    I spent years studying how users actually behave online — where they click, where they hesitate, where they leave. I learned to read analytics dashboards the way a doctor reads test results: looking for patterns, diagnosing problems, and prescribing targeted fixes.

    Today I work with business owners, coaches, and digital brands who want to stop guessing and start growing. My approach is simple: measure everything, trust the data, and make changes that actually move the needle.
